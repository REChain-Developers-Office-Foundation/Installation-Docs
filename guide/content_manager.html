# Content Manager

In the 'Content Manager' section, you have the capability to add apps from both the Google Play Store and the Apple App Store. You can utilize the search box to search for apps. The search results also display the app's addition status, such as 'Added' or 'Not Added,' helping you avoid re-adding apps you've already added.

# Scraper Categories

Scraper Categories allow you to match the categories from both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store with the categories on your website. You can simply leave any categories unselected that you do not wish to retrieve from the respective app markets.

# About APK Files

Please note that the software is unable to retrieve APK files from the Google Play Store. This is a result of changes in Google Play Store's access methods and security measures. Retrieving APK files, which was possible years ago, has now become increasingly challenging. Major APK websites use server-based shell scripts or desktop applications to emulate mobile devices and download such files, and this process is quite complex.

# Important Information Regarding the Apple App Store

For the Apple App Store, it's important to note that its search mechanism requires you to search for the exact target word. For instance, searching for 'game' will not yield any results, but searching for 'Grand Theft Auto' or 'GTA' will display the results you're looking for. It's essential to understand that the software utilizes the Apple App Store website for app searches, which means that the results you see may differ from those in the App Store app itself.
