# Submissions

On the Submissions page, you'll find a list of apps submitted by visitors. You can add submitted apps to your site or remove those that don't comply with your policies.

If you do not wish to allow visitors to add apps, you can uncheck the 'Enable App Submission Form' in the Settings page.
